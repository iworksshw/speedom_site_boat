Misa Motorboat Racing Park

Motorboat Racing Park

We provide guidance to help you conveniently enjoy the park.
Misa Motorboat Racing Park was established for the rowing and canoe events of the 1986 Asian Games and the 1988 Seoul Olympics. Centred around the rowing lake, the park features sports facilities such as football and foot volleyball fields, convenience facilities including shaded areas, kiosks, and bicycle rental, as well as recreational facilities, making it a beloved leisure destination for citizens.
